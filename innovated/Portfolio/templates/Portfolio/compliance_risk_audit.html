What is a Compliance Risk Audit?

A Compliance Risk Audit is a systematic assessment of an organization's adherence to labour laws, statutory regulations, internal policies, and industry standards.

We conduct labour law due diligence and vendor audits to evaluate compliance health, identify operational gaps, and recommend proactive corrective actions to minimize legal and financial risks.

Purpose & Outcomes

Purpose

  • Verify adherence to labour laws & regulations
  • Assess compliance with internal company policies
  • Evaluate workplace safety & employee welfare standards
  • Review data privacy & financial compliance controls
  • Identify operational & statutory compliance risks

Outcomes

  • Clear understanding of compliance posture
  • Identification of compliance gaps
  • Risk prioritization & mitigation strategies
  • Actionable corrective recommendations
  • Improved operational transparency

What to Expect from an Audit

Organizations are required to prepare statutory documents and participate in operational interviews during the audit process.

  • Collection & verification of compliance documents
  • Employee & management interviews
  • Vendor compliance assessments
  • On-site compliance inspections
  • Gap analysis & process evaluation
  • Final audit report with prioritized recommendations
  • Corrective action planning support
