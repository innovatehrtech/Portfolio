What are Establishment Compliances?

Establishment compliances refer to the statutory and legal obligations every business must fulfill to operate smoothly and legally. These compliances include registrations, labor law adherence, workplace safety measures, statutory filings, employee welfare, licenses, inspections, and maintenance of mandatory records.

Proper compliance management helps organizations avoid legal penalties, improve employee satisfaction, and maintain smooth business operations.
