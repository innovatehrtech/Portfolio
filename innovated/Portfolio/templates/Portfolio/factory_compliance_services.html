What is Factory Compliance?

Factory compliance refers to the legal and statutory obligations that manufacturing units must fulfill under the Factories Act and related labor regulations to ensure safe, healthy, and compliant workplace operations.

Proper compliance management helps factories reduce operational risks, improve employee safety, avoid penalties, and maintain uninterrupted production.
